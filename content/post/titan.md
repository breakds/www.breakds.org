让我牵起你的手，轻轻放进我的口

吮吸咀嚼浑不够，毕竟一年没吃肉

### English Translation

Let me take the hand from you

And tenderly put it into my mouth

Enjoying the delicious food

Since I haven't been able to taste the meat for so long
